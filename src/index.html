How to Play Jigsaw Master

  • Drag & Drop: Move jigsaw pieces from the piece pool tray onto the board to assemble the image.
  • Magnetic Snapping: Bring correct neighboring pieces close, and they will snap and group together automatically!
  • Peek Eye (): Press and hold the eye button at the top to preview the completed puzzle.
  • Grid Helper (): Tap the grid button to toggle a background outline map of where all pieces should go.
  • Shuffle Tray (): If you have too many pieces stuck underneath each other in the tray, hit shuffle to clean them up.
